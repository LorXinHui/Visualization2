EMPLOYMENT AGRICULTURE INDUSTRY SERVICES

This dataset includes the percentage of persons employed by sex and ISIC 4 categories of economic activity, namely agriculture, industry and services.

EMPLOYMENT OF EACH ECONOMIC ACTIVITIES BY GENDER

Among the 4 categories of economic activity, approximately 50.4% of the world population is employed in the sector of services in Year 2020. The sector of services is also the highest employed sector for both gender.

TREND OF EMPLOYMENT FOR EACH ECONOMIC ACTIVITIES

The percentage of people employed in the sector of services and industry has been increasing since year 2005. The increase of employment in industry is especially obvious for males,
while the employment for females has remained about the same throughout the years.

Dated since 2005, we see an overall decline in agriculture employment for both gender.

Burundi, officially known as the Republic if Burundi is a small country in east-central Africa. This petite country with a total area of 27,834 km2 holds approximate 11,865,821 citizens. Among the 11M citizens, 92% are employed in agriculture. 96% of the females and 87.8% of males are involved in the agriculture industry.

It is undeniable that Africa has the highest population percentaged involved in the sector of agriculture. Agriculture being the main economic source of Africa contributes to 3.6% of increase in their GDP.

Macao, officially known as the Macao Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China, is actually a city of China instead of a country. It holds a total land area of 115.3km2 with 682,300 citizens. 90.1% of its population is employed in the field of services, among which includes 97.4% of its female population and 83.3% of male population.

Not only is it the city with highest population percentage in the field of services, it is also one of the cities with highest per capita income in the world.

The employment of services has been increasing gradually since Year 2005 with approximately half of the world population employed in Year 2020. Northen America holds the highest population percentage employed in the sector of services, which is approximately 79% regardless of gender.
